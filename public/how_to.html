DYNAMIC VISUALIZATION

There will be two visualization object on the page, a DAG on top and a treemap below.

On the DAG object :

  • A blue, green or orange rectangle is an interaction with the treemap.
  • A orange rectangle represents the root of the tree.
  • A green rectangle represents the representatives.
  • A blue rectangle represents the terms of interest of the CSV files.
  • The yellow rectangles represents the intersection terms between those previous mention terms.

1. General visualization of the directed acyclic graph at the level 0 (links root to representatif terms).

2. General visualization of the directed acyclic graph at the level 2 (link root to terms).

On the treemap object :

  • There is three levels for this visualization.
  • First, the representatives are displayed.
  • Second level (when we click on one of the repsentatives of the first level), all the terms connected to this representatives are displayed
  • Third level represent all the genes connected to the term selected on the seconde level.

There is interactions from the DAG to the treemap, by clicking on the colored rectangles, and from the treemap to the DAG.
